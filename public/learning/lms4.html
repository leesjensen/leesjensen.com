LMS 4.0

With the introduction of AI and LLMs, we have the opportunity to revolutionize learning management systems. The next wave will successfully provide the following.

  1. Authoring assistance that simplifies the creation of courses and learning materials, enabling educators to focus on pedagogy rather than details.
  2. Personalized content that is dynamically created. The LMS will leverage the online experience to provide dynamic, interactive, and personalized learning content. This will include generated assessments, instruction video with interactive paths, textual instruction, and self paced learning.
  3. Feedback and grading will be automated and personalized, leveraging AI to provide timely and constructive feedback to learners, as well as adaptive grading systems that reflect individual progress and mastery.
  4. Learning through creating a course. The learner will be able to create a course as a way to demonstrate mastery of a subject, and the LMS will provide tools and support for this process.
  5. Alerting mentors and students when progress is impeded or when intervention is needed to support learner success. Content and remediation is automatically created.
